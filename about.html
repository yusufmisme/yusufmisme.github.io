About Our Team

Learn about our journey, our mission, and the amazing students and mentors who make our team successful.

Our Story

Founded in 2011, our robotics team began with just a handful of students and a passionate teacher who believed in the power of hands-on STEM education.

Since then, we've grown to 8 members and have participated in 3 seasons, winning 7 awards and placements along the way.

Our journey has been defined by the FIRST core values: Discovery, Innovation, Impact, Inclusion, Teamwork, and Fun in everything we do.
