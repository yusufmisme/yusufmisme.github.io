Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the team?

We welcome new members at the beginning of each school year. Middle or High School Students are to prove they are hardworking and must live in the San Antonio area. Contact us directly to apply.

What competitions do you participate in?

We compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), an international high school robotics competition where teams design robots to complete unique challenges each year.

How can businesses sponsor the team?

We offer various sponsorship levels with different benefits. Please contact us at 5189wolvesrobotics@gmail.com for more information.

Can you visit our school/event?

Yes! We love sharing our passion for robotics with the community. Please use the contact form above to request a demonstration or booth.
