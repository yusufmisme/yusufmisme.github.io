Our Outreach

Inspiring the next generation of innovators through community engagement and STEM education.

Our Outreach Mission

Wolves Robotics strives to be an ambassador for FIRST and FTC. We make sure to spend time at community service and outreach events, and bring STEM closer to our community.

Our team made it a goal to take every opportunity to inspire the youth by sharing the benefits of FIRST programs and the knowledge we gained participating in them.

We do this by posting about our STEM experience on social medias such as Instagram, Discord, and YouTube, as well as participating and setting up outreach events!

Recent Outreach Events

Onshape Summer Camp

July, 2024

We hosted a month long camp over the summer at TheSchool of Science and Technology, open to local teams, where we taught howto use Onshape to design astarter robot for the season.

Representing First in Texas, Fiesta de los Niños

April 20, 2024

Wolves Robotics introduced the robotics programs available through FIRST in Texas to curious youth visiting the Boeing Center for Fiesta de los Niños event.

Discovery Day at the Doseum

January 20, 2024

We introduced FTC to youth at a local museum, The Doseum, for Discovery Day event. Our team interacted with youth to display aspects of our robotics experience.
